sloppyjoes

Description

Sloppy Joes are a classic American comfort food.This hearty sandwich features ground beef simmered in a tangy, sweet tomato-based sauce and piled high on a soft bun. It's a beloved dish that's perfect for a casual meal.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • onions
  • Milk
  • tomato-based sauce
  • hamburger bun

Steps

  1. Cook the beef, onion, and green pepper.
  2. Drain the liquids, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Simmer for about 30 minutes. Serve on hamburger buns.
