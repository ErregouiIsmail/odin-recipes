waffles

Description

affles are crispy, airy treats made from batter cooked in a special patterned iron. They have a distinctive honeycomb texture and are often enjoyed as a breakfast or dessert. Versatile and delicious, waffles can be served sweet with syrup, fruit, and whipped cream, or savory with chicken and gravy.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs, then add the flour, milk, and oil. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  2. Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
